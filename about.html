About Alif Investigations

Alif Investigations was founded by Mostafa Hassan, a former United Nations Internal Investigator, Ontario Assistant Crown Attorney, and current Criminal Duty Counsel. With over a decade of experience conducting and prosecuting complex investigations, Mostafa established Alif Investigations to provide employers with a trusted, independent resource for resolving sensitive workplace matters.

Mostafa’s professional timeline — from international tribunals to the Canadian justice system — reflects a consistent commitment to fairness, integrity, and cultural understanding. That journey, outlined below, forms the foundation of Alif’s approach: impartial, legally informed, and deeply respectful of the people and organizations involved.

A Personal and Accessible Approach, Not a Process Factory

When you hire Alif, you work directly with Mostafa. He handles every step — intake, interviews, and the final report — with no juniors or subcontractors. This ensures consistent, discreet, and fully accountable service.

Alif is not a law firm. Our sole focus is fact-finding, fairness, and resolution, providing a process that is clear, respectful, and approachable for organizations of all sizes — including nonprofits, community groups, and large employers alike.

Independence You Can Trust

As a current Criminal Duty Counsel, Mostafa is professionally restricted from providing legal advice to employers. This distinction safeguards true independence and impartiality, reinforcing your organization's reputation for transparency and integrity. Retaining Alif signals that your company's priority is truth, no defence — an important message to both employees and regulators.

Flexible, Responsive, and Cost-Effective

Alif Investigations recognizes that smaller organizations and nonprofits often lack the resources to engage large law firms or consultancy practices. Our model is designed for accessibility and flexibility, offering evening and weekend availability to reduce disruption and accommodate tight timelines. Every inquiry receives a direct and timely response from Mostafa himself, ensuring a clear and efficient process.

Legally Informed, Culturally Sensitive

With professional experience spanning the United Nations, the International Criminal Court, and Canada's justice system, Mostafa brings a deep understanding of procedural fairness and evidence-based investigation.

As a professional who has worked with diverse international teams, he approaches every matter with cultural sensitivity and empathy—qualities essential for navigating today's multicultural workplaces with care and accuracy.
